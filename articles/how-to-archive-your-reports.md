# Archive a report

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/reports/how-to-archive-your-reports/
**Category:** Reports

---

This article will show you how to archive your reports and utilize this feature for your needs.

**Navigating to the Reports Screen**

  * **Log in** to your **Comparative Agility account**
  * Open **Reports**



_The screenshot below will show you where to find the report screen_

  * 


**Archive a report**

Archiving the reports has never been easier. To do it, simply:

  * Click on the **three dots** next to the specific report
  * Locate the **Archive report** button
  * Your report will be archived instantly
