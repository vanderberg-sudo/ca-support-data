# Managing Capabilites

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/managing-capabilites/
**Category:** General

---

Managing capabilities in Comparative Agility allows you to customize and fine-tune your assessment process according to your organization's needs. This article provides a comprehensive guide on how to manage capabilities effectively.

### **Content**

  * Capabilities Screen
  * Enabling or Disabling Capabilities
  * Data Categories
  * Apply Maturity Frameworks
  * Configure Capability
  * Create New from Template
  * Preview Statements
  * Exploring Capability Details
  * Download Questions
  * Export data



### **Navigating to the Capabilities Screen**

  * Open **Settings**
  * Click on **Capabilities**

### **Enabling or Disabling Capabilities**

To differentiate enabled and disabled capabilities, enabled ones will be indicated by a blue color, while disabled capabilities will be grayed out.

_The short video below shows you how to enable/disable capabilities_

**Note:** Only enabled capabilities will be seen when you want to add a new survey and send it out.

### Data Categories

The Data Categories allow survey creators to define categories like location, preference, or role when setting up a survey. If you would like to include Data Categories into a specific capability, simply enable the ones you prefer.

_In the screenshot below we show you how to add categories to the specific capability_

To learn more about data categories, please visit our Help Center article [**Creating and Managing Data Categories**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/2-advance-topics/how-to-create-and-manage-data-categories/).

### Maturity Framework

The maturity framework offers a structured approach to assess and track team progress. By defining and visualizing maturity levels, teams gain a clearer understanding of their capabilities and areas for improvement.

_The screenshot below shows you where to find the Maturity Framework option_

To learn more about it, please visit our Help Center article [**Maturity Framework**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/2-advance-topics/maturity-framework/).
