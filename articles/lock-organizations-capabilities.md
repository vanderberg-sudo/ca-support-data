# Lock organization&#8217;s capabilities

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/lock-organizations-capabilities/
**Category:** General

---

This article will guide you on how to lock an organization's capabilities. With this feature, you are restricting users from enabling capabilities and only permitting access to pre-approved ones.

**Navigating to the Capabilities screen**

  * Open **Settings**
  * Click on the **Capabilities**



_In the short video below we show you how to navigate to the Capabilities screen_

**Lock Capabilities**

To lock all capabilities, simply:

  * Within the **Capabilities screen** navigate to the **Lock** icon
  * **Click** on it
  * All the capabilities will be **locked** instantly



_The short video below shows you how to lock capabilities_

**Note** : Only the account owner and super admin can lock the capabilities if not defined otherwise.

To learn more about custom roles and their available features, please visit our Help Center article [**Custom Roles**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/administration/custom-roles/).

**Unlock Capabilities**

In case you would like to unlock capabilities, follow these steps:

  * Within the **Capabilities screen** navigate to the **Lock** icon
  * **Click** on it
  * All the capabilities are now **unlocked** and users can enable and disable them
