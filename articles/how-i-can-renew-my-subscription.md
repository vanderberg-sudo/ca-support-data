# How can I change my subscription?

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/billing/how-i-can-renew-my-subscription/
**Category:** Support &amp; Billing

---

This article will guide you through renewing and canceling your subscription. Your account billing date is set on the day you first buy a subscription and it will be renewed automatically on the expiration date.

**Navigating to the Subscription Section**

  * Open **Settings**
  * Locate the **Subscription** and click on it



_In the short video below we show you how to navigate to the subscription section_

**Upgrade your subscription**

If you would like to purchase more teams, simply:

  * While on the **Settings Screen** , scroll down to the **Subscription Section**
  * Click on the **Upgrade plan** option
  * Type in the **requested information**
  * Proceed with the **Continue** button



_The short video below shows you how to purchase more teams_

**Cancel your subscription**

In case you decide to cancel your subscription, please follow the steps below:

  * While on the **Settings Screen** , scroll down to the **Subscription Section**
  * Click on **Subscription**
  * Select the **Cancel Subscription** option
  * Proceed with the **Confirm** button
