# Activating my free SAFe subscription

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/how-do-i-activate-my-free-subscription-as-a-safe-community-member/
**Category:** Get started

---

As part of your SAFe community membership, you will get FREE access to 1 team in Comparative Agility. This article will guide you through activating it in a few simple steps.

**Navigating to the SAFe Studio**

To create an account, please navigate to the [**SAFe Studio**](https://safe.scaledagile.com/en/home) and log into your account using your credentials.

**Activating your subscription**

In order to activate your subscription, please make sure that you are logged into your SAFe Studio account.

Follow the steps below for activation:

  * Open the **navigation menu** on the left-hand side
  * Click on **Practice** to open the **dropdown menu**
  * Choose **My SAFe Assessments**
  * Under the **SAFe Measure & Grow Partner** section locate **Comparative Agility**
  * Click on **Learn more**
  * Proceed with the **Click Here to Get Started** button
  * Type in the **information** and proceed with the **Continue** button
