# External Client

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/2-advance-topics/external-client/
**Category:** Advanced Topics

---

This article will show you the process of connecting as an external client to securely access and interact with our API. This is valuable for organizations looking to automate data exchange, enhance system interoperability, and streamline their operational workflows.

**Navigating to the Integrations screen**

  * Open **Settings**
  * Click on **Integrations**



_The short video below shows how to navigate to the Integrations screen_

**External Client**

To acquire your unique credentials, begin by enabling the **External Client**. The client ID and client secret will be used later for authorization. Once copied, navigate to the **Comparative Agility API documentation** by clicking on it, which will open a new tab. Lastly, scroll to **Auth,** paste your keys in the corresponding **Value fields** , and click on **Execute** to complete the process.

By referring to our external **API documentation** , you can effectively evaluate our endpoints using the **client ID** and **client secret** available in the Integrations screen under Settings. This resource will guide you on the necessary request headers, requirements for each API endpoint, and the corresponding responses from each endpoint.

Comparative Agility offers **6 API resources** :

  * **Capabilities**
  * **Instances**
  * **Personal Users Data**
  * **Roles**
  * **Teams**
  * **Users**
