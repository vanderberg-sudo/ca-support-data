# What is the Product Owner PI tool?

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/personal-improvement/what-is-the-product-owner-pi-tool/
**Category:** Personal Improvement

---

This tool features a self-assessment survey with progress tracking, tailored recommendations for enhancing key Product Owner skills, and a Community Board for collaboration.

With this tool, CSPOs can evaluate their strengths and pinpoint areas for learning and growth as Product Owners.

**Key Features**

  * **Self-Assessment Survey:** Take a role-specific survey that tracks your progress and identifies areas for improvement.
  * **Curated Recommendations:** Get personalized suggestions to focus on specific growth areas.
  * **Community Board:** Engage in discussions and share insights with peers and experts for further learning.



**Getting Started**

To get started, you will take one of our assessment instruments. These assessments have been written by domain experts, and have been verified by data scientist

**Reviewing Results**

After completing the assessment, you’ll receive a clear overview of your performance across critical dimensions, along with personalized recommendations.

**Personal Backlog**

This journey is at your own pace. When you’re ready to act on any suggestions, add them to your personal improvement backlog to keep track of your progress. To explore more recommendations:

  * Click the **plus sign** next to the statement
  * Find the **recommendation**
  * Click the **plus sign** **button** to add it to your backlog



**Tracking Progress Over Time**

Keep an eye on your skill development across all critical dimensions. Regularly retake the survey to assess your progress.

**Community Engagement**

Got a question or a topic you want to explore further? Join the Community Board to start discussions, connect with peers, and learn from experts to deepen your understanding.
