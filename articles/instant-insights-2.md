# Creating and Managing Instant Insights

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/instant-insights/instant-insights-2/
**Category:** Instant Insights

---

This guide will show you how to create and manage surveys using the Instant Insights feature. With Instant Insights, you can gather quick, anonymous feedback from your team, ask them anything, and view their real-time responses. It's the perfect tool for collecting instant data and gaining valuable insights!

**Creating questions and answers**

First and foremost, navigate to the **Instant Insights Screen** and click on the **New Survey** button to begin.

_The short video below shows you how to start a new survey_

To change the question type, simply click the **dropdown menu** and select your preferred option. Also, you can enter your questions in **multiple locations** , such as the **main screen** or within the **Question field**. Just click the field that works best for you and start typing your question.

The same process applies to **adding answers**. Whether on the **main screen** or within the **Answer field** , click the field and enter your responses. For certain question types, you can also **rearrange the answers** by moving them **up** or **down** to get the order just right.

_In the short video below we show you how to create questions and answers_

**Note** : To add a new question, simply click on the **New Question** option in the upper left-hand corner.

To learn more about creating a survey, please check out our Help Center article [**Creating Instant Insights Surveys**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/instant-insights/creating-instant-insights-surveys/).

**Question settings**

By clicking on the **three dots** within the question box, you'll have several options. You can **move the question** to a different position, **duplicate** it, or **delete** it entirely. This gives you flexibility in managing the structure of your survey.

_The short video below shows you the question settings_

**Style your survey**

You can **customize the appearance of your survey** to suit your preferences. Several styling options are available, allowing you to select different **layouts** that can include or exclude **images** or **background colors**.

Additionally, you have control over **text styling** , including options to adjust the **color** , **font size** , and the **size of the answer text**. This ensures your survey looks just the way you want it.

_In the short video below we show you how to style your survey_

**Presenting the survey**

Once you are ready to present a survey, there are two different ways:

  * **Right after creating a Survey**
    * **Name** your survey
    * You can proceed with the **Save** button and follow the steps below or
    * Click on the **Present** button
    * **Copy and share the link** with participants or let them **Scan the QR code**
    * **Start the Survey** once everyone is present



_The short video below shows you how to present the survey right after creating it_

  * **While on the main Instant Insight Screen**
    * Click on the **three dots** under the Survey you would like to present
    * Choose the **Present** option
    * **Copy and share the link** with participants or let them **Scan the QR code**
    * **Start the Survey** once everyone is present



_In the screenshot below we show you how to present the survey from the main instant insights screen_

**Note** : The preview button allows you to see exactly how your survey will appear before you save or present it. This gives you the chance to make any adjustments and ensure everything looks perfect.

To learn more about this option, please visit our Help Center article [**Start a Survey**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/instant-insights/start-a-survey/).

**How do participants see the survey?**

After sharing the link or scanning the QR code, a new tab will open, displaying the question and added statements. Participants can easily make their selection by clicking on the desired statement and then clicking **Submit**.

**Main Instant Insights screen**

The **main screen** offers a variety of options. In addition to **creating a new survey** , you can click on the **three dots** under any survey you've created to access additional actions. These include **presenting the survey** , **viewing the report,** **duplicating** it, **renaming it** , **sharing the report,** or **deleting** the survey entirely.

_In the screenshot below we show you the main instant insights screen_

**Instant Insights report**

Once you are done with the survey, a report will be created instantly for you. To access it, simply:

  * Go to the **Main Instant Insights Screen**
  * Click on the **three dots** under the completed survey
  * Select **View Report**



_The screenshot below shows you how to access the report_

On the **Report Screen** , you have several options to manage and share your results. You can **export the report** , **save it as a PDF** , **share** it with others, and, if available, **change the language of the report**. These features make it easy to customize and distribute your survey findings.
