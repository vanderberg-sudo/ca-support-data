# Feedback 360

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/feedback-360/feedback-360/
**Category:** Feedback 360

---

This article will guide you through our new platform, 360-degree feedback. It is a process that gathers input from managers, peers, and direct reports to provide a well-rounded view of an employee’s performance and development needs.

**Add a survey to the portfolio**

  * On the **main dashboard** , locate the **survey**
  * Click on the **\+ icon**



_In the short video below we show you how to add a survey to the portfolio_

**Creating Custom 360**

You have the ability to create your own **Custom 360** and send it out to gather feedback. To do that, follow these steps:

  * Click on the + sign next to **Create Custom 360** or in the upper **right corner**
  * Add **Dimensions** , **Characteristics,** and **Statements**
  * Once ready, click on **Save**



_The short video below shows you how to create a Custom 360_

To learn more about creating custom capabilities, please visit our Help Center article [**Editor**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/editor/).

**Initiating the existing survey**

To start with the process of gathering feedback, simply:

  * Click on the **Initiate Survey**
  * Either **Preview Questions** or click on **Next**
  * Choose between the two sharing options: **Invite by email** or **Generate links**
  * Go through all **options**
  * Send out the **survey**



_In the short video below we show you how to initiate the survey_

**Note** : If you would like to **close the current** and **start the new survey** , please proceed with the **Initiate Survey** option in the upper right corner.

_The screenshot below shows you how to start the new survey_

To learn more about the Invite by email option, please visit our Help Center article [**Feedback 360 - Invite by email**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/feedback-360/feedback-360-invite-by-email/); or to learn more about the links, please visit our Help Center article [**Generate Links for Feedback 360**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/feedback-360/feedback-360-general-links/).

**Start Self-Assessment**

In order to see additional insights, you should take the assessment yourself. Once you send out the survey, you can take it whenever you want. Simply:

  * Open the **specific assessment 's tab**
  * Click on the **Start Self-Assessment**



_In the short video below we show you how to start self-assessment_

**Report**

Once you gather feedback, you will be able to open a report for more details, and to do that, simply click on the **View Report.**

_The screenshot below shows you where to find the report_

By clicking on the three dots next to the selected survey, you have the ability to **Preview Questions** or **Remove the survey from the Portfolio**.
