Welcome to Comparative Agility's enhanced onboarding experience – the **Starter Guide**! This intuitive tool is designed to be your go-to companion as you explore the diverse features within Comparative Agility. Whether you are a new user or a seasoned pro, the **Starter Guide** adapts to your journey, providing valuable insights and guidance tailored to your current screen.

**Activating the Starter Guide**

  * Navigate to **any screen** in Comparative Agility
  * Click on the **Info** icon in the upper right corner
  * **Toggle on** the **Starter Guide** option



_In the short video below we show you how to activate the Starter Guide_

**Understanding the Starter Guide**

The Starter Guide, also known as the onboarding tool, is your personal navigator within Comparative Agility. It is more than just a set of instructions; it is a dynamic guide that evolves with you as you move through different screens in the platform.

  * **Intuitive Guidance -** The Starter Guide kicks in automatically based on your current screen in Comparative Agility. It recognizes where you are and offers guidance on the features and functionalities relevant to that specific context.
  * **Adaptive Content -** Say goodbye to generic onboarding! The Starter Guide provides context-specific information, ensuring that you receive the most relevant and actionable guidance tailored to your immediate needs.



#### **Making the Most of the Starter Guide:**

  * **For New Users -** Leverage the Starter Guide to familiarize yourself with Comparative Agility's interface. It is like having a personal tour guide to introduce you to each screen's functionalities.
  * **For Seasoned Users -** Even if you are well-acquainted with Comparative Agility, the Starter Guide remains a valuable resource. It can help you discover new features, refresh your memory on existing ones, and stay informed about recent updates.
