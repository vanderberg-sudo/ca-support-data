# How to get started: Agile Alliance

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/how-to-get-started-agile-alliance/
**Category:** Get started

---

As part of your Agile Alliance membership, you will get FREE access to 1 team as well as the Premium version of Instant Insights. This article will guide you through activating it in a few simple steps.

**Navigating to the Agile Alliance**

To create an account, please navigate to the **Agile Alliance** website and log in using your **credentials**.

**Activating your subscription**

In order to activate your subscription, please follow the steps below:

  * Open the **Membership** dropdown menu
  * Select **Product/Service Providers**
  * Locate **Comparative Agility** and open the product
  * Click on the **Get Offer Now**
  * Proceed with the **Sign Up Now**
  * Type in all necessary information and **Sign Up**



_In the short video below we show you how to activate your subscription_

**Note** : All Agile Alliance members are eligible to receive a Comparative Agility subscription to conduct Instant Insights assessments as well as 1 team.

To learn more about how to utilize Instant Insights, please visit our Help Center article [**Creating and Managing Instant Insights**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/instant-insights/instant-insights-2/).

If you have any questions or need assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team at **support@comparativeagility.com** and we will be more than happy to help you.
