# Using Tags In Reports

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/reports/using-tags-in-reports/
**Category:** Reports

---

In this article, we will show you how to utilize, add, and remove tags on your reports. This is a powerful feature as it allows you to easily sort and search for your data.

**Navigating to the Reports Screen**

  * **Log in** to your **Comparative Agility account**
  * Open **Reports**



_The screenshot below will show you where to find the report screen_

**Create a tag**

  * Locate the **desired report**
  * Click on the **three dots** (or right-click on the report)
  * Select **Add tags**
  * **Add a new tag** or **remove the existing one**
  * If you add a new tag, click on the **Enter** **button** on your keyboard



_In the video below we show you how to create a tag_

**Search by tags**

This feature allows you to easily sort and search for your data.

  * Navigate to the **Search bar**
  * Search report by **name** or**tags**



_The short video below shows you how to search for a report_

**Delete a tag**

If you would like to delete a tag, simply:

  * Locate the **desired report**
  * Click on the **three dots** (or right-click on the report)
  * Navigate to the **x icon** next to the specific tag
  * Your tags will be deleted instantly
