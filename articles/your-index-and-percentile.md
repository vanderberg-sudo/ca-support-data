# Your Index and Percentile

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/your-index-and-percentile/
**Category:** General

---

Your Index and Percentile show how your organization’s adaptability and performance compare to others. These numbers come from the World and Industry Indexes, which use data from thousands of teams around the world. This gives you a better understanding of how your team performs in different assessments compared to others.

**Your Index**

Your Index measures how your team performs relative to others in terms of flexibility, innovation, and efficiency.

**Percentile Calculation**

Your Percentile shows where your organization stands. For example, being in the 80th percentile means you perform better than 80% of organizations.

**How It 's Calculated:**

  * **Data Scores** : Performance is assessed based on responses and how answers are distributed
  * **Weighted Questions** : Some questions carry more weight to ensure precise and context-specific reporting
  * **Global Comparison** : Responses are measured against anonymized data from organizations globally



**What Your Index Means:**

  * **Higher Scores** : Your organization is effectively applying best practices compared to others
  * **Lower Scores** : There are opportunities for improvement in your organization's adaptability and approach



Tracking Your Index over time allows you to monitor progress, identify growth areas, and remain competitive in a constantly evolving environment.
