# Personal Improvement &#8211; Getting started with PI

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/personal-improvement/personal-improvement/
**Category:** Personal Improvement

---

The Personal Improvement module is an application focused on helping you grow in your role by providing you with targeted recommendations, a view of personal progress, and a personal improvement backlog. This tool is designed to facilitate continuous improvement and mastery in your chosen field.

Personal improvement is a confidential self-assessment instrument designed by domain experts that will identify how well you are doing in a given professional area, suggest helpful resources for improvement, and provide a supportive community for continuous growth.

**Getting started**

To get started, you will take one of our assessment instruments. These assessments have been written by domain experts, and have been verified by data scientists. To find an assessment you would like to take, simply:

  * Open**Marketplace**
  * Locate the assessment you like and click on **Add to your portfolio**
  * Click on **Take a survey**



_The short video below shows you how to take a survey_

**Note** : The answers are private and confidential and will not be viewed by anyone else unless you choose to share the results with a mentor.

**Results**

When you have completed the assessment, we will provide you with an intuitive overview of how you are doing across critical dimensions related to the topic and several recommendations will appear.

These curated resources are targeted specifically based on your responses. Reviewing these recommendations and following the guidance will help you grow in the areas where it is most important to you.

The **pie chart** provides a visual representation of your results. For a more detailed analysis, simply click on a **result** , and it will display how you answered each question.

_In the video below we show you how to navigate to the results_

Note that you can review each of the recommendations and choose which ones to turn into action items.

**Sharing your results** can be done in just a few clicks:

  * Select a **Coach sharing** option
  * Click on **Add coach/advisor**
  * **Add email address** and click **Done**



_The short video below shows you how to share your results_

Note that your **coach/advisor** must be registered on this site.

**Backlog**

This is a journey we will take at your pace. When you decide to take action on some of the items, make sure to add them to your personal improvement backlog to ensure you keep track of your progress. To list more recommendations, click Load more recommendations. To add a recommendation, simply:

  * Click on the **plus sign** next to the statement
  * Locate the **recommendation**
  * Click on the **plus sign button** next to the recommendation



You can also add your own notes here, for instance, if you discover something worth digging deeper into or discussing with the community.

Once you have completed an item, mark it done by clicking on the **checkbox**.

_In the short video below we show you how to add recommendations to the backlog_

Additionally, if you find something interesting that you would like to add to your backlog, you can add it as a new recommendation:

  * Click on **Add New Recommendation**
  * **Name it**
  * **Paste the link** and click on **Create Recommendation**



_The short video below shows you how to Add New Recommendations_

**Progress over time**

Monitor how your skills are progressing broken down by all the critical dimensions within your context. On a regular basis, take another survey to check your results this time around.

**Show World Index** is an option that you can enable and compare your progress with the world's index.

_The video below shows the Progress over time screen_

On the progress over time timeline, you will be able to track your results consistently and even see how you are doing compared to your peers.

**Community**

Do you have a question or a topic you would like to discuss further? Head on over to the community and start the conversation. Leverage the power of your peers, like-minded professionals, and experts to get to the bottom of things.

To create a new post, follow these steps:

  * Open **Community screen**
  * Click on **New post**
  * Fill out the **title** and **description**
  * Address it to the **public** or **experts**
  * Enable the **Anonymous option** if needed
  * Choose from the **tags** available (optional)
  * And **Save**



_In the short video below we show you how to create a new post_

Note that **experts** are domain experts with **demonstrated experience** and **deep knowledge** in this particular topic. If experts are chosen, only these thought leaders will see your question.
