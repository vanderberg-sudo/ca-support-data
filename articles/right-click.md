# Right Click

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/right-click/
**Category:** General

---

The recent update to Comparative Agility introduces a powerful new feature: the **right-click option**. This enhancement consolidates various functionalities across the application, making them easily accessible with a simple right-click of your mouse. While this is a developing feature, its integration is already available in the main screens and primary features of the application.

To utilize this feature, simply **right-click** anywhere within the application interface. A contextual menu will appear, providing you with a range of options tailored to the screen you're currently using.

_In the short video below we show you the right-click option_

**Note** : The right-click option is available on the Structure, Reports, Custom Capabilities, and Users screens.

### Benefits of the Right Click

  * **Streamlined Navigation - **The right-click option eliminates the need to navigate through multiple menus or tabs, allowing you to access key functionalities directly from the screen you're on.


  * **Efficient Workflow - **Tasks that were previously spread out across the app are now centralized, promoting a more efficient workflow.


  * **Intuitive Interface - **The contextual menu is designed to intuitively present options relevant to the content or section you're working in, making your experience more user-friendly.
