# Joining Your Company Account in Comparative Agility: A Step-by-Step Guide

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/joining-your-company-account-in-comparative-agility-a-step-by-step-guide/
**Category:** Get started

---

Comparative Agility is a powerful platform that allows teams and organizations to gather and analyze data for continuous improvement. When invited to join a Comparative Agility account, the process differs slightly depending on whether you're an SSO (Single Sign-On) user or a regular user.

**Regular Users**

  * **Open** your email inbox
  * Locate the **email**
  * Click on **Join** button
  * This action will redirect you to the **Comparative Agility platform**



To learn more about adding new users as an admin, please visit our Help Center article [**Add new users**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/add-new-users/).

_In the screenshot below we show you how to accept the invite as regular user_

Once completed, you are all set to explore and utilize Comparative Agility for your team or organization's needs.

**SSO Users**

  * **Open** your **email inbox**
  * Locate the **email**
  * Click on the **Join** button
  * This action will redirect you to your **organization 's Comparative Agility account**



_The screenshot below shows you how to accept the invite as SSO user_

**Note:** Enter your SSO credentials (if required) and authenticate your access through your organization’s system before clicking on the join button in the email.

If you ever need any assistance or have any questions please reach out to our support team at **support@comparativeagility.com** and we will be more than happy to assist you.
