# Accessing Scrum Master PI and Product Owner PI

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/personal-improvement/accessing-scrummaster-pi-and-product-owner-pi/
**Category:** Personal Improvement

---

Access to the ScrumMaster PI and Product Owner PI tools within Comparative Agility requires valid CSM and CSPO certifications respectively, below are instructions on how you can access the **[Personal Improvement tool](https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/personal-improvement/).**

**Product Owner PI Tool**

  * Visit the **[Product Owner PI Tool](https://resources.scrumalliance.org/Article/comparative-agility-product-owner-personal-improvement-tool)** link
  * **Log in** to your **Scrum Alliance account**
  * Upon logging in, you will **gain access** to the **Comparative Agility Product Owner PI Tool**



To learn more about this tool, please visit our YouTube video [**The Product Owner Personal Improvement Tool.**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LFQQcW4AoBg&t=86s)

**ScrumMaster PI Tool**

  * Visit the [**ScrumMaster PI Tool**](https://resources.scrumalliance.org/Article/comparative-agility-scrummaster-personal-improvement-tool) link
  * **Log in** to your **Scrum Alliance account**
  * Upon logging in, you will **gain access** to the **Comparative Agility ScrumMaster PI Tool**



To learn more about this tool, please visit the Vimeo video [**ScrumMaster Personal Improvement Tool**](https://vimeo.com/438947123).

If you ever need any assistance or have any questions please reach out to our support team at **support@comparativeagility.com** and we will be more than happy to assist you.
