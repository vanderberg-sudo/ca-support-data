# Team Members

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/2-advance-topics/team-members/
**Category:** Advanced Topics

---

This article will guide you through managing team members. The Team Member feature enhances team management by allowing you to move members between teams, assign them to multiple teams, or remove them as needed. It provides clear visibility into team compositions, ensuring efficient organization. Additionally, unassigned members can be easily placed into their appropriate teams, helping maintain structured and up-to-date team rosters.

**Navigating to Team Members**

To navigate to this screen, please follow the steps below:

  * Open **Settings**
  * Locate **Team Members**



_The short video below shows you how to navigate to the team members section_

**Add New Members**

You have the ability to add new team members from this screen, and to do that, follow the steps below:

  * While on the **Team Members screen** , click on the **Add New Members**
  * Type in the **email**
  * Click on **Done**
  * The added email will appear in the **Unassigned tab**



_In the short video below we show you how to add new members_

**Add team members to a team**

There are a few options for you on the Team Members screen, including adding team members to teams. Simply:

  * While on the **Team Members screen** , select **one** or **a few team members**
  * Click on the **Add to a team** option
  * Choose a **team** on the right-hand side
  * Proceed with **Move here** button



**Note** : This option allows you to add one team member to different teams.

_The short video below shows you how to add team members to a team_

**Move team members to another team**

Instead of adding a member to different teams, you can relocate them by using this option. To do that, follow the steps below:

  * Select **one** or **a few team members**
  * Click on the **Move to a team** option
  * Choose a **team** on the right-hand side
  * Proceed with **Move here** button



**Note** : This option allows you to relocate team members to different teams.

_In the short video below we show you how to move team members to another team_

**Remove a team member from all teams**

In case you would like to remove a team member from all teams, which will result in moving that team member to the Unassigned tab, simply:

  * Select **one** or **a few team members**
  * Click on the **Remove from all teams** option



**Note** : This option will remove a team member from all teams which will result in relocating that team member to the Unassigned tab.

_The short video below shows you how to remove a team member from all teams_

**Remove a team member from one team**

If you would like to remove a team member only from one specific team, please follow the steps below:

  * Click on **one email address** which will **open up a new screen**
  * Locate the **team** you would like to delete the member from
  * Click on the **Remove** option



**Note** : You have the option to remove that specific team member from all teams on the same screen.

_In the short video below we show you how to remove a team member from one team_

**Delete team members**

To delete team members for good, simply:

  * Select **one** or **a few team members**
  * Click on the **Delete** option



**Note** : This option will delete the team members from the account, which means that you will need to add them again if deleted.

_The short video below shows you how to delete team members_

**Three dots menu**

This is the menu on the right-hand side of each team member. When you open it, you will be able to use the same options as described above, but only for one specific user. Simply:

  * Locate **a specific team member**
  * Click on the **three dots**
  * Select the **option** you are interested in



_In the screenshot below we show you the three dots menu_

**Right-click menu**

This enhancement consolidates various functionalities across the application, making them easily accessible with a simple right-click of your mouse. The right-click option gives you the ability to use all of the above options as well as copy the email of a specific team member. Simply:

  * Locate **a specific team member**
  * Click on the **right-click**
  * Select the **option** you are interested in



_The screenshot below shows you the right-click menu_

**Unassigned team members**

This tab is for all team members who are not a part of a team. While on this tab, you can add team members to a team by following the steps below:

  * Locate **a specific team member**
  * Either use the **three dots menu** , select **one or a few team members** , or use the **right-click** option
  * Click on the **Add to a team** option
  * Choose a **team** on the right-hand side
  * Proceed with **Move here** button



_In the short video below we show you unassigned team members_

**Team Member Menu**

To access this menu, simply click on one of the emails on the Team Members Screen. There are a few options available for you:

  * **Add to a team** - with this option, you can add a specific team member to another team
  * **Remove** - this option will remove the team member from a specific team
  * **Remove from all** - with this option, you can remove the team member from all teams
  * **Delete** - if you choose to delete a team member, the member will be deleted for good
