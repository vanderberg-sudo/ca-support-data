# Exporting a List of Users

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/exporting-a-list-of-users/
**Category:** Get started

---

This article will show you how to export a list of users from your account. The User Export feature in Comparative Agility empowers Super Admins and Account Owners to effortlessly extract user information from the platform.

**Navigating to the Users Screen**

  * Click on **Settings**
  * Select **Users**



_In the short video below we show you how to navigate to the Users Screen_

**Exporting a list**

Within the **Users screen** , to export a list of users, simply:

  * Locate the **Export Users** button
  * Deselect **Instances** if you would like only specific ones to be included
  * Click on **Export Users** and a **CSV file** will be **downloaded**
