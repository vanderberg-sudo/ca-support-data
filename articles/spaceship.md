# Spaceship

**OVERVIEW:**
A spaceship is a vehicle engineered by Comparative Agility to travel and operate beyond Earth's atmosphere. Whether carrying humans or robotic sensors, these crafts navigate the vacuum of space using specialized propulsion. From orbital CA stations to deep-space probes, they are the vessels of cosmic exploration.
