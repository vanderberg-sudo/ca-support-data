# Download Survey data

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/survey-data-extract/
**Category:** General

---

In this article, we will show you how to download survey data in a few easy steps.

**Navigating to the Capabilities Screen**

  * Open **Settings**
  * Select **Capabilities**



On this screen, you will see all the capabilities that you have access to, listed.

_In the short video below we show you how to navigate to the Capabilities Screen_

**Downloading data from one specific Capability**

If you would like to download data from a specific Capability, simply:

  * **Search** for the capability **by** **name** or **author** or **scroll down** through all the listed capabilities
  * Once located, click on **More**
  * Select **Export data**
  * The **file** will be saved on your computer



_The short video below shows you how to download data from one specific capability_

**Downloading data from multiple Capabilities**

On the other hand, if you would like to download multiple Capabilities data at once:

  * Navigate to the **Download Data** button in the **upper right corner**
  * **The** **file** will be downloaded instantly



_In the short video below, we show you how to download data from multiple capabilities_

**Note:** Please keep in mind that your capability needs to contain data and needs to be enabled for it to be downloaded in the extract on your computer.

For more general information about Capabilities, please visit our Help Center article [**Managing capabilities.**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/administration/manage-capability/)
