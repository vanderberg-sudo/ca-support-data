Leveraging the world's largest dataset of teams in agile transformation, Elite 8 sets a new standard for performance comparison in the industry. Elite 8 allows you to benchmark yourself against the best of the best. Previously, teams could compare their performance to the global average, gaining insights into their standing relative to the broader community. With Elite 8, you now have the ability to put your performance against top-performing teams, providing a more granular and insightful comparison.

Navigating to **Elite 8** is easy:

  * Simply access the **analysis screen** and locate the **Elite 8** section
  * From there, you can **create your report** and uncover where you stand relative to the best of the best
