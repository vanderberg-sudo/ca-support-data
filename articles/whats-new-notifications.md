# What&#8217;s New Notifications

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/whats-new-notifications/
**Category:** General

---

"What's New" Notifications are timely alerts that inform you about the latest developments and updates in Comparative Agility. These notifications highlight **new features, improvements, news, webinars, courses, new assessments, and innovations** that can enhance your experience and drive organizational success. By keeping you in the loop, we aim to create a seamless and evolving environment tailored to your needs.

**Navigating to the "What's New" Notifications**

  * **Log in** to your **Comparative Agility account**
  * Navigate to the **" What's New"** section in the upper right corner
  * **Read through the notifications** to learn about new features, enhancements, and innovations



_In the video below we show you how to navigate to the "What's New" Notifications_

With "What's New" Notifications, you can stay informed about the latest features and innovations, optimize your experience, and embrace continuous improvement. Explore the "What's New" section and harness the power of Comparative Agility's advancements to drive your organization's success.

If you ever need any assistance or have any questions please reach out to our support team at **support@comparativeagility.com** and we will be more than happy to assist you.
