# Trends Report

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/reports/trends-report/
**Category:** Reports

---

In the fast-paced realm of modern business, data-driven insights reign supreme. Enter the Comparative Agility Trends Report, your compass for navigating the agile landscape. This report unveils trends, providing invaluable insights for businesses striving to excel in today's dynamic world.

The Comparative Agility Trends Report offers a holistic view of your organization's agility. It's a snapshot backed by data and analytics, empowering decision-makers to identify strengths and areas for improvement.

Located at the top right, the report offers an average view of selected data. This benchmarking tool illuminates where you excel and where growth is needed. There is also the World Index feature that offers global context, comparing your performance on a larger scale.
