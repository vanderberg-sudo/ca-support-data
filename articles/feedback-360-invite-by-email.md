# Feedback 360 &#8211; Invite by email

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/feedback-360/feedback-360-invite-by-email/
**Category:** Feedback 360

---

This article will guide you through sending out 360 surveys by using the Invite by email option. Feedback 360 is a process that gathers input from managers, peers, and direct reports to provide a well-rounded view of an employee’s performance and development needs.

**Initiating the survey**

To start with the process of gathering feedback, simply:

  * Click on the **Initiate Survey**
  * Either **Preview Questions** or click on **Next**
  * Choose the **Invite by email** option
  * Select the **deadline**
  * Add **participants ' emails** and **roles**
  * Proceed with the **Finish** option



_In the short video below we show you how to use the email option_

**Add participants**

If you would like to add new participants and send them email invites, simply:

  * Open the **survey tab**
  * Click on the **three dots**
  * Select **Add participants**
  * Type in the **emails** and choose their **roles**
  * Click on **Add**



_The short video below shows you how to add new participants_

**Invitations**

Comparative Agility gives you the ability to see all the invitations you sent, as well as whether someone has already responded.

  * Open the **survey tab**
  * Click on the **three dots**
  * Select **View Invitations**
