# Statements Screen for individual survey

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/statements-screen-on-team-level/
**Category:** General

---

This article will help you update your surveys on an **individual level** with a few clicks. The**Statements Screen** is a place where you can select/deselect statements, enable/disable survey comments or add the end of survey comments, change and update survey mode and style, and additionally, change the language of the survey for the particular team.

**Navigating to the Statements screen**

  * Open the **Structure Screen**
  * Find the **survey** you would like to apply the changes to or **create a new one**
  * Click on **Statements** on the chosen survey



_The short video below shows how to navigate to the Statements screen on an individual survey level_

**Select/deselect statements**

This feature allows you to effortlessly select the specific questions you want to share with your team. Should you wish to make adjustments to your survey, you can easily do so by following the same steps. What is advantageous is that there is no necessity to send a new link once the survey is updated.

_In the short video below we show you how to select/deselect statements_

**Enable/disable the End of Survey comment**

Turning on the End of Survey comment gives the participants a chance to leave an optional comment at the end of the survey. This is a powerful feature for gathering crucial feedback from your participants.

_The screenshot below shows where to find the enable/disable End of Survey comments option_

**Enable/disable Optional comment**

Comments in surveys can be super helpful for getting feedback from the people taking your survey. If you turn on the option for optional comments, everyone can share their thoughts on each survey statement while they are going through it.

_In the short video below we show you where to find the enable/disable optional comments option_

**Randomize Questions**

Comparative Agility allows you to randomize survey questions, making repeated survey distribution more engaging. By presenting statements and dimensions in a different order each time, this feature helps maintain interest and reduces response fatigue.

_The screenshot below shows you where to find the randomize questions option_

**Anchors Direction**

With this option, you can choose if the anchors will be in the descending, ascending, or random order.

_In the screenshot below we show you where to change anchors direction_

**Languages**

Should you prefer to send the survey in a language other than the default, you can effortlessly do so by selecting the language that best suits your needs.

_The screenshot below shows you where to find the languages option_

**Survey style**

With this feature, you can easily modify the format and appearance of your survey. Whether you prefer to present it as a list or a single item, you can update your preferences with just one click.

To read more about customizing your survey, please visit our Help Center article [**Customize your Survey**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/customize-your-surveys/).
