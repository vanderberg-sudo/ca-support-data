# Creating Custom Capability

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/creating-custom-capability/
**Category:** Get started

---

This article will guide you through creating a custom capability, which will give you a fully customized experience for all your needs in terms of creating a survey. Besides custom surveys, we have a variety of genuine surveys, created by domain experts and validated by data scientists.

The Custom Capability feature is located on the left-hand side of your screen.

_The screenshot below shows you where to find the Custom Capability screen_

  * **Use sentences or keywords** - a powerful new way to build your custom capability. Use smart keywords and semantic search to instantly find and add the most relevant survey statements.



_In the short video below we show you how to access the sentences and keywords feature_

To learn more about this feature, please visit our Help Center article [**Search Statements**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/2-advance-topics/search-statements/).

  * **Start with existing capability** - start with one of our genuine surveys, then combine it with others—or add your own custom capabilities. Leverage this option to create surveys that truly fit your needs.



_The short video below shows you how to start your custom capability with the existing survey_

To learn more about all the options that we are offering, please visit our Help Center article [**Editor**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/editor/).

**Note** : Another way of creating a custom capability from one of our genuine surveys is by using the option **Create New from Template** on the **Capabilities Screen**.

  * **Start with a blank canvas** - start from a blank canvas and shape your survey from the ground up. Add only what you need—mix in genuine surveys or your own custom capabilities to craft a survey that fits your purpose perfectly.



_In the short video below we show you how to use the blank canvas feature_

To learn more about the blank canvas option, please visit our Help Center article [**Editor**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/editor/).

If you ever need any assistance or have any questions please reach out to our support team at **support@comparativeagility.com** and we will be more than happy to assist you.
