# Activate your premium Comparative Agility plan from Scrum Alliance dashboard

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/2-advance-topics/activate-your-premium-comparative-agility-plan-from-scrum-alliance-dashboard/
**Category:** Advanced Topics

---

This article will guide you through activating your Free Premium Comparative Agility account. The plan is provided as part of our strategic partnership with Scrum Alliance and is available for all CSP’s and CTC’s, CEC’s, CST’s…

To **create an account and activate the premium free CA plan** , please follow the steps below:

  * Visit [**this page**](https://www.scrumalliance.org/login?ReturnURL=https%3A%2F%2Fcertification.scrumalliance.org%2Fdashboard)
  * Log in using your **Scrum Alliance credentials**
  * Open **Benefits**
  * Locate the **Comparative Agility Assessment Tool** benefit
  * Proceed with the **Continue** button
  * Type in **the** **necessary information** and click **Continue**
  * Your account is now created and ready for use!
