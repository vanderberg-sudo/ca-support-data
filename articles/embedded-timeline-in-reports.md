# Embedded Timeline in Reports

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/reports/embedded-timeline-in-reports/
**Category:** Reports

---

#### **Overview**

The Embedded Timeline is a new feature in Comparative Agility reports that lets you explore how your team or teams have evolved over time — directly within the report.

Instead of switching to the Trends view, you can now move through time from inside the report itself. With one click, you can instantly see how results change from one assessment to another.

  * This makes it easier to:
  * Track progress across multiple assessments
  * Compare past and current states
  * Understand how transformation efforts are impacting teams



#### **What Is the Embedded Timeline?**

The Embedded Timeline appears at the top of reports and displays a timeline of completed assessments.

Each point on the timeline represents a snapshot in time. When you select a date, the entire report refreshes automatically to show the data from that moment.

This gives you a living view of team evolution, without leaving the report.

#### **How It Works**

  1. Open a report for a team that has completed the same assessment multiple times over a period of time.
  2. At the top of the page, you’ll see the timeline bar with available assessment dates.
  3. Click on any point in time on the timeline.



You are now viewing the results exactly as they were at that point in time.
