# Satisfaction Index Survey

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/satisfaction-index-survey/
**Category:** General

---

This article will guide you through the steps of using the Satisfaction Index survey. Promoter Feedback survey measures how satisfied customers are with a company’s products, services, or overall experience. By gathering feedback on key aspects of customer interactions, the CSI provides a clear score that helps identify areas for improvement and track satisfaction levels over time.

**Instant Insights**

With Instant Insights, you can gather quick, anonymous feedback from your team, ask them anything, and view their real-time responses. It’s the perfect tool for collecting instant data and gaining valuable insights!

Comparative Agility offers various templates, among which you can find the Satisfaction Index - Promoter Feedback assessment. To access it, simply:

  * Open **Instant Insights**
  * Click on **See All** in the upper right corner
  * Choose a **template**
  * Proceed with the **Use Template** button



_In the short video below we show you how to access Instant Insights templates_

To learn more about instant insights, please visit our Help Center article [**Creating and Managing Instant Insights**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/instant-insights/instant-insights-2/).

**Genuine Survey**

Another way of gathering feedback from the customers is sending out one of our Genuine Surveys. Once you create a structure, and you are ready to start sending out surveys, please follow the steps below:

  * While on the **Structure Screen** , locate the **team** you would like to add the survey to
  * Click on **Add new capability**
  * Choose the **Satisfaction Index Survey**
  * Click on the **Preview** button if you would like to update it
  * Once, ready, click on the **Proceed** button



To learn more about how to create folders and teams before sending out the surveys, please visit our Help Center article [**Creating Structure**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/how-to-create-the-structure/).

To learn more about this topic, please visit our Help Center article [**Sending Surveys**](https://help.comparativeagility.com/1-how-to-get-started/sending-surveys/).

**Satisfaction Index in Reports**

No matter whether you used Instant Insights or Genuine Survey, the report will follow the same format. It will include all the questions and answers along with a calculated satisfaction index.

**Create your own Satisfaction Index Survey**

  * Open **Custom Capability**
  * Choose the **Start with a Blank Canvas** option
  * **Type in** your statement
  * Convert it to the **Satisfaction Index**



_The short video below shows you how to create a satisfaction index survey_

You can also enable optional comments. This option will give the respondents the possibility to add a comment tied to a specific statement. To enable it, please follow the steps below:

  * Navigate to the **General** button
  * Click on the **Enable Optional Comments** option



_In the screenshot below we show you how to enable optional comments_

Additionally, create mandatory/optional comment fields. If you would like to have a mandatory/optional comment field that the respondents should fill out, simply:

  * Select the **statement**
  * Change the **Question Type** to **Open-ended**
  * Choose if you would like to make it **optional** or **mandatory**
