# Delete a report

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/reports/how-to-delete-a-report/
**Category:** Reports

---

This article will show you how to delete a report in the Reports Screen.

**Navigating to the Reports Screen**

  * **Log in** to your **Comparative Agility account**
  * Open **Reports**



_The screenshot below will show you where to find the report screen_

**Deleting a report**

To delete a report, simply:

  * Click on the **three dots** next to the specific report
  * Locate **Delete** button
  * Confirm it by selecting the **Delete** button
