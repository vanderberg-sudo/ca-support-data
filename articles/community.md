# Personal Improvement &#8211; Community

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/personal-improvement/community/
**Category:** Personal Improvement

---

Leverage the power of your peers, like-minded professionals, and experts. Get hints, tips, and practical advice. 

**Navigating to the Community**

  * **Log in** to your **Personal Improvement account**
  * Navigate to the **Community** screen



_In the screenshot below we show you where to find the Community screen_

**Create new post**

  * Click on **New post**
  * Fill out the **title** and **description**
  * Address it to the **public** or **experts**
  * Enable the **Anonymous option** if needed
  * Choose from the **tags** available (optional)
  * Proceed with the **Save** button



_The short video below shows you how to create a new post_

**Note:** Experts are domain experts with demonstrated experience and deep knowledge in this particular topic. If you choose to address your question to the Expert community, only these thought leaders will see your question. 

**Browse various posts**

You will be able to browse other people's posts and engage with them. Simply:

  * Within the **Community** screen, **scroll** through the posts
  * **View comments** or **reply** to them
