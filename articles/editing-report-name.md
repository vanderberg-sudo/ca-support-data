# Editing Report Name

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/reports/editing-report-name/
**Category:** Reports

---

This article will guide you through editing the report name.

**Navigating to the Reports Screen**

  * **Log in** to your **Comparative Agility account**
  * Open **Reports**



_The screenshot below will show you where to find the report screen_

**Edit report name**

To edit the report name, simply:

  * Click on the **Edit name** button
  * Update the **name**
  * Press **Enter** on your keyboard to save
