# You already answered this survey ! (What to do?)

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/billing/you-already-answered-this-survey-what-to-do/
**Category:** Support &amp; Billing

---

If the message "**You already answered this survey !** ", as in the picture below, pops up for one of your team members, don't worry we have a quick solution for you.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

**This is what you can do.**

  1. Select your capability and click on 'Copy Link' option located on the right side menu.
  2. Now you can share that link with the person that needs to respond to your survey.



<https://help.comparativeagility.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/rmlBS0KwBW.mp4>

 

**Why do I see this message ?** ("_You already answered this survey !_ ")

If you see this message, someone already submitted the response to your survey using a particular link, such as a corporate mailbox or group email address.

When you use our email option to share surveys, we create a unique link for each email address. Once you receive a response, we disable additional submissions using that link. We do this to limit one person from responding to a particular survey multiple times.
