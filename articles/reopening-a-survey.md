# Reopening a Survey

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/reopening-a-survey/
**Category:** General

---

This article will guide you through reopening a survey. Reopening surveys can be a valuable tool for refining your assessments and gathering the most accurate data possible.

**Navigating to the Structure Screen**

  * **Log in** to your **Comparative Agility account**
  * **Structure Screen** will be opened as a default screen



_In the screenshot below we show you where to find the Structure Screen_

**Reopen a Survey**

  * Locate the **team** for which you would like to reopen a survey
  * Navigate to the **desired Survey**
  * Click on the **Show Older Surveys** button
  * Select the **Lock** button
  * Proceed with the **Confirm** button
  * Choose between **various options** to **share** your survey



_The short video below shows you how to reopen a survey_

**Note:** You will be able to reopen the last survey.

If you ever need any assistance or have any questions please reach out to our support team at **support@comparativeagility.com** and we will be more than happy to assist you.
