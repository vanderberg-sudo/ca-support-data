# Do you have any surveys for Kanban?

**URL:** https://help.comparativeagility.com/3-general/do-you-have-any-surveys-for-kanban/
**Category:** General

---

Our [Agility survey](https://www.comparativeagility.com/capabilities/Ca) is focused on Scrum, but we would say that about +90% of all questions should be relevant to many other agile practices. As an example, I would ask Kanban team members to take the survey but just disregard the following two questions:  
- Whole teams, including the ScrumMaster and Product Owner, have no more than 12 people on them.  
- All work is done in iterations of no more than 30 days.

Also, it might be a good idea to review the questions with team members and decide what questions are not applicable to them. If they feel a question is not relevant in their context, simply instruct them to select “Not Applicable” and the response will not be part of the analysis.
